Rentıng Terms

  2. You can pay your Transfer fee to our driver in EFT - bank transfer or cash.
  3. Our site has the right to update transfer pricing.
  4. Reservations made through our site are confirmed by our company as soon as possible and gain certainty.
  5. Any changes that may occur during the Transfer time, location, or flight time must be communicated to us via WhatsApp or by phone.
  6. No extra charge will be charged for delayed plane landings of up to an hour and a half.
  7. If the pet is to be transported, the pet must be transported in the cage.
  8. You must be at the airport 1 hour before your domestic flights and 2 hours before your international flights.
  9. According to the laws; tobacco, cigarettes, and alcohol cannot be consumed in our vehicles.
  10. There are no baby seats in our vehicles. The baby seat that passengers bring with them must be connected by our guests following the laws and company policy.

  13. You must complete your information in the booking form.
  14. Please check that you have entered your mobile phone and email address fully and correctly. otherwise, our company cannot be held responsible for problems that will be encountered.
  15. Our transfer prices are valid per car, not per person.
  16. Please check that the number is complete and complete when placing your luggage in our vehicles. Our staff will help you with the transportation of luggage. Baggage above the vehicle baggage capacity specified on our website will not be accepted into vehicles.
  17. Wheelchairs, bicycles, electric bicycles, surfboards, ski kits and golf bags not notified at the time of booking will not be accepted for vehicles.
  18. At airports, you are greeted at the exit door by our welcome officer or driver with a sign with your welcome name written on your reservation.
  19. The prices indicated on our website include vehicle, driver, fuel and Highway charges. (Private expressways and bridges are not included.)
